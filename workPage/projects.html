Projects

  • Project IMPACT is a program to provide high school students, who have demonstrated academic talent, especially in mathematics and problem-solving, with an opportunity to explore some of the ways that college students and professionals think and work in computer science and information systems. This opportunity is for students who have not experienced computer science and are undecided about their academic and career goals. The one day program will give participating students the chance to investigate areas in which they have potential talent.


  • Siena ACM-W Club is a campus Women in Computing club.
